Frequently asked questions

What is Multi-Location Inventory Tracking?

Multi-Location Inventory Tracking allows you to monitor and manage inventory across various storage locations or retail outlets from a single platform, ensuring consistent stock levels and visibility.

How can Multi-Location Inventory Tracking benefit my business?

This feature enhances inventory accuracy, reduces the risk of overstocks and stockouts, and improves overall supply chain efficiency by providing real-time insights into every location's stock levels.

What are Inventory Transfer Suggestions?

Inventory Transfer Suggestions are automated recommendations generated by our system to optimize stock levels across locations. They help ensure that inventory is distributed efficiently to meet demand without surplus or deficit.

Can I see the inventory levels for all locations?

Yes, our system updates inventory visibility for each location, enabling you to make informed decisions quickly and respond to changes in demand or supply conditions.

How does the Demand Forecasting by Location work?

Demand Forecasting by Location uses historical sales data and predictive analytics to estimate future product demand at each location, helping you prepare inventory accordingly and avoid potential sales losses.

What kind of reports can I generate with this feature?

You can generate reports such as Inventory Levels by Location, Inventory Transfer Suggestions, and Demand Forecasting by Location, which provide comprehensive insights into your inventory management across multiple sites.

Can Inventory Transfer Suggestions thresholds be customized?

Yes, you can customize the thresholds and parameters for generating Inventory Transfer Suggestions based on your specific business needs and inventory management strategies.
